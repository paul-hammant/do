Paul Hammant's DevOps

I am the high level IT consultant with C-level experience that comes into your organization to assess your DevOps programs or DevOps readiness so that you can best target your expenditures for people and resources and make every dollar count and can optimize your DevOps outputs.

As a DevOps Assessor, I arrive at a corporate client site, ask a whole bunch of questions, look at systems, apps, infra, code, and come up with recommendations for that client. I am always going to focus on the lower tiers of the DevOps layer cake first, because they facilitate more advanced aspects, and are almost always needing attention.

I then work with the team to execute a plan to get them to their goals. That can be locally or from afar. I will also help you present everything to execs, sell the economic benefits, cite the case studies.

I have performed many assessments over the years. At the beginning, 12 years ago, those were Agile assessments, then Continuous Delivery assessments as techniques emerged, an latterly DevOps assessments. Many of those were as a Principal consultant at the famous ThoughtWorks consultancy, but more recently some have been as a solo operator.

I have presented findings to CEOs and CIOs and executive teams. I have seen clients say ‘yes’ to everything I have recommended, and implement them. I have also seen clients struggle to implement changes as their commitments and constraints put pressure on them to maintain the status quo. I have crafted my practice and honed my approach after tens of these assessments. I also do follow-ups to help measure progress towards goals, and provide further advice, for clients that want to run the execution of the plan on their own.

I have brief case studies for an airline, an FX trading bank, a fin-tech startup.

You should know that I:

  • lead the creation of the info portal TrunkBasedDevelopment.com
  • am the person who’s written most about Trunk-Based Development, generally
  • am the first person to write about Branch by Abstraction
  • am able to help you move past simple Trunk Based Development and towards Google’s Monorepo style.
  • know how to massively reduce your test automation times, which helps throughput
  • co-created the industry standard web testing technology, Selenium, back in 2004
  • co-created the first Dependency Injection Container that used Constructor Injection in 2003

I am based in New York, but frequently travel to client sites in the USA. I also have clients outside the USA.
